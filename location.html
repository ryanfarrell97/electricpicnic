About Stradbally

Stradbally, a town in Co. Laois, has hosted the annual festival from its creation in 2004. Located close to Portlaoise, one of the major towns in the midlands, it is an ideal location for a festival of this kind. With trains and buses regularly serving the nearby towns of Portlaoise and Arlington, the festival is easily accessible, with there being different hotels around the county of Laois, which are covered in a different page on the website.
